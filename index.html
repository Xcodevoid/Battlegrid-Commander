📖 BattleGrid Command Instructions

💰 Economy System

  • Each unit has a price based on its stats
  • Sandbox Mode - Both teams start with equal money
  • Campaign Mode - Earn money by winning battles
  • Higher levels in Campaign = more money but stronger enemies!

🎮 Controls

  • Select a Unit - Click on a unit type from the left panel
  • Select Team - Choose Red Team or Blue Team
  • Place Units - Click on the battlefield to place your units
  • Start Battle - Press "Start Battle" to begin the fight!

🟡 Territory System

  • Yellow Line - Divides the battlefield in half
  • Red Team - Can ONLY place units on the LEFT side
  • Blue Team - Can ONLY place units on the RIGHT side
  • If you click on the wrong side, you'll get an error message!

🏆 Game Modes

  • Sandbox Mode - Free play with custom settings
    • Choose your map
    • Set max units per team (1-100)
    • Set starting money (100-5000)
  • Campaign Mode - Progressive battles
    • Select difficulty level (1-500)
    • Higher levels = more enemies & stronger units
    • Win to progress, lose and retry!

⚔️ Unit Types

  • Infantry - Balanced melee units (Knight, Warrior, Soldier, Pikeman)
  • Ranged - Attack from distance (Archer, Crossbowman, Longbowman)
  • Magic - Powerful spellcasters (Mage, Wizard, Sorcerer)
  • Healers - Support units that heal allies
  • Heavy Melee - High damage fighters (Barbarian, Berserker, Assassin)
  • Cavalry - Fast mounted units (Horseman, Knight Rider, Cavalry)
  • Defense - Tanky units (Guardian, Sentinel, Wall, Bunker)
  • Special - Mythical creatures (Dragon, Phoenix, Golem, Demon)

📊 Unit Stats

  • HP - Health Points (more = more durable)
  • ATK - Attack damage per hit
  • RNG - Attack range (melee vs ranged)
  • SPD - Movement speed
  • Heal - Healing ability (for healer units)

🎯 Tips & Tricks

  • Combine ranged units with melee frontlines
  • Use healers to keep your frontlines alive
  • Cavalry is great for flanking and catching archers
  • In void maps, position carefully or your units will fall!
  • Special units (Dragon, Golem) are expensive but powerful
